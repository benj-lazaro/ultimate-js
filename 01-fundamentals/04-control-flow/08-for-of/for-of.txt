For..of
-------
- The for...of statement executes a loop that operates on a sequence of values
    - Sourced from an iterable object (e.g. Array, String, Map, etc.)
    - Introduced in ES6
    - Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Statements/for...of)


Syntax:

for (variable of iterable)
  statement
