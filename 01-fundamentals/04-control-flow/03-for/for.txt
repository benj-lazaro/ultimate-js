For
---
- The for statement creates a loop that consists of three optional expressions
        - Initialization or Initial Expression
        - Condition 
        - Afterthought or Increment Expression or Decrement Expression

    - Enclosed in parentheses and separated by semicolons
    - Followed by a statement (usually a block statement) to be executed in the loop
    - Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Statements/for)


Syntax:

for (initialization; condition; afterthought)
  statement


- JavaScript have several loops
    - For
    - While
    - Do..While
    - For..in
    - For..of

    - They all perform the same thing
        - To repeat an action a certain number of time
        - But there is a subtle difference on how they start & end a loop
