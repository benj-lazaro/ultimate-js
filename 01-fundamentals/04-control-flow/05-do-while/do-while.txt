Do..while
---------
- The do...while statement creates a loop that executes a specified statement 
    - As long as the test condition evaluates to true
    - The condition is evaluated after executing the statement
        - Resulting in the specified statement executing at least once

    - Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Statements/do...while)


Syntax:

do
  statement
while (condition);


- The difference with the while loop
    - The do..while loop gets executed once
