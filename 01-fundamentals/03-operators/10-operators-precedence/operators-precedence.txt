Operators Precedence
--------------------
- Determine how operators are parsed concerning each other
    - Operators with higher precedence
        - Become the operand of operators with lower precedence

    - When working with complex compressions
        - Use a pair of ( )
        - To control / determine how operators are applied

    - Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Operators/Operator_Precedence)
