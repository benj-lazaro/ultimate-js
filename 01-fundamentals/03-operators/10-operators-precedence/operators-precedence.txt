Operators Precedence
--------------------
- It determines how operators are parsed concerning each other
    - Operators with higher precedence become the operands of operators with lower precedence
    - Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Operators/Operator_Precedence)

    - When working with complex expressions
        - Use a pair of parenthesis ( ) to determine how the operators are applied
